Advanced Features for a Next-Level Arbitrage Platform
1. Real-Time Social & News Sentiment
Integrate social media, news, and financial tweet feeds side-by-side for tracked tickers.

Sentiment heatmaps in charts (“Buy interest rising,” “Bearish trend in social feeds”).

2. Smart Alerts & Notifications
Conditional alerts: "Alert me if X and Y together."

WhatsApp, e-mail, and mobile push integrations for trade triggers.

3. Automated Arbitrage Execution
Broker API integration (e.g., Zerodha/Kite, Interactive Brokers) for one-click trade execution or autotrading bots.

Simulated and real modes side by side.

4. Risk Simulation & Scenario Analysis
“What if” trade simulator with adjustable volatility, capital, or spreads.

Monte Carlo simulation visualizations for long-term strategy/risk.

5. Personalized AI Recommendations Dashboard
Per-user or per-account opportunity feed, based on AI-learned trading style/preferences.

Explainable AI: “Why this trade now” with natural language reasoning.

6. Multi-Asset & FX Arbitrage
Add crypto, US stocks, or forex—unified dashboard, cross-market arbitrage.

Correlation matrix tool for inter-asset opportunities.

7. Institutional Analytics
Portfolio risk dashboard (beta, Sharpe, VAR, Max Drawdown, diversification score).

Market impact estimates—“This trade might move the price X%.”

8. Pro Reporting & Automation
PDF, Excel, and e-mail reports: daily PnL, trade logs, risk summaries in one click.

Custom export APIs and webhooks for pro users.

9. Theme Customization, Light/Dark/Auto Modes
Allow user theme presets (“Luxury,” “Terminal Green,” “Stealth Black”).

Accessibility color grades.

10. Team/Trading Desk Collaboration
Share opportunity lists or trade notes in real time with teammates.

Slack/Discord/Teams integration.

11. Keyboard Shortcuts & Voice Control
Power user: “Open backtest”, “Switch to NIFTY chart” with keystrokes.

Voice command: “Show arbitrage opportunity for TCS”.

12. Visual AI Assistant (LLM Copilot)
Chat-driven command palette (“Plot this stock, summarize last 30m, show risk table”).

Let the AI write custom backtest scripts for the user.

13. Deep Dives and Pattern Recognition
AI identifies rarely-seen spread patterns (“Statistical anomaly: mean-reverting spread on INFY”).

Interactive annotatable charts—draw, highlight, tag notes.

14. Mobile Companion App Integration
QR code login, instant alert push/simulated orders from a phone.

15. API Marketplace Integration
Broker/plugin marketplace for third-party signals, social copy trading, data providers.
